About Me

Hi, I'm Enoch Arddy

I'm a professional photographer and videographer with over 10 years of experience capturing special moments and telling compelling visual stories.

My journey began with a simple passion for capturing the world around me, which evolved into a career dedicated to creating meaningful visuals for clients across the globe. I believe that every moment has a story worth telling, and I strive to capture the essence of each subject through my lens.

I specialize in wedding photography, commercial projects, and documentary-style videography. My approach combines technical expertise with artistic vision to deliver results that exceed expectations.
